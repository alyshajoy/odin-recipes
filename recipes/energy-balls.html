Energy Balls

Description

Delicious and nutritious snack!

Ingredients

  • oats
  • sunflower butter
  • honey
  • coconut
  • flax seed
  • chocolate chips
  • chia seeds

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Roll into balls.
  3. Place in the freezer for an hour.
  4. Transfer to a ziplock bag.
