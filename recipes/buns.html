Hamburger Buns

Description

Some of the easiest and most delicious hamburger buns you've ever tasted!

Ingredients

  • water
  • sugar
  • yeast
  • olive oil
  • egg
  • flour

Steps

  1. Proof yeast in water.
  2. Mix in egg and olive oil.
  3. Slowly knead in flour.
  4. Leave to rise for one hour.
  5. Shape into buns.
  6. Rise 20 more minutes.
  7. Bake at 375 for 16 minutes.
