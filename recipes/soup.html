Gnocchi Soup

Description

A delicious homemade soup that even my kids like!

Ingredients

  • italian sausage
  • gnocchi
  • red pepper
  • onions
  • garlic
  • spinach
  • basil
  • broth

Steps

  • Cook sausage in the oven.
  • Saute all veggies.
  • Add sausage to the cooked veggies.
  • Add in basil, salt and pepper.
  • Pour in broth, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Add spinach and gnocchi, and cook for 5 more minutes.
